Open Staff Positions


Are you a high school, college undergraduate, or college graduate student looking to become involved with Postscript? Below are descriptions for open positions on the Postscript editorial board and staff:


ART EDITORS | Fine Art Correspondent
Focuses on reviewing art submissions in the fine arts each reading period through Submittable

POETRY READERS
Reviews poetry submissions each reading period through Submittable

PROSE READERS
Reviews prose submissions each reading period through Submittable

BLOG CORRESPONDENTS
Manages the Postscript Journal blog, writing weekly editorial pieces to be published online; topics ranging from arts and culture to interviews of contributors

MANAGERIAL STAFF | Director of Public Relations
Facilitates the intricacies of maintaining an in-print and online literary magazine

MANAGERIAL STAFF | Social Media Correspondent
Works with Postscript’s social media platforms to keep Postscript at the literary forefront

MANAGERIAL STAFF | International Outreach Correspondent
Works with international organizations and young writers in collaboration

MANAGERIAL STAFF | Website Design Correspondent
Updates and organizes the Postscript website (HTML/CSS)
